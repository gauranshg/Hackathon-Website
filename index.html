Rules And Regulations

  • Maximum team size is 3 members
  • This hackathon is meant to be participated by undergraduate students.
  • Cross college team participation is allowed.
  • There is absolutely no registration fees.
  • All the submission prior to the final rounds will be conducted Online.
  • The final round will be conducted in Hybrid mode.
  • Teams are encouraged to join us offline at our campus for the final round, but it is not compulsory.
  • Teams are allowed to choose any problem statement of their choice.
  • Teams need to work on their solution during the time of Hackathon. Any previously worked on projects will be ruled out of the judging process.
  • We have an eminent panel of judges. Hence, their decision will be considered above all.
